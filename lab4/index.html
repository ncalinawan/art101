How to Use a Dryer:
1. Open the dryer door.
2. Locate the lint trap.
3. Remove the lint trap.
4. Dispose of the lint inside.
5. Replace the lint trap.
6. Put wet clothes into the dryer.
7. Put one or two dryer sheets inside, depending on load.
8. Close the dryer door.
9. Adjust dryer settings as desired.
10. Start the dryer.
